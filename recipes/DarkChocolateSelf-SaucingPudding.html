Dark Chocolate Self-Saucing Pudding

Ingredients

  • 165 grams dark (70%) chocolate, roughly chopped
  • 170 grams unsalted butter, chopped
  • 6 eggs
  • 165 grams raw caster sugar
  • 115 grams plain flour
  • 60 grams almond meal
  • Cocoa powder and Thickened cream [to serve on the side]

How to Pudding

  1. Preheat oven to 200 C degrees. Grease a 1.25L baking dish. Place chocolate and butter in a small saucepan over low heat adn cook for 3-4 minutes or until melted. Remove from heat and cool completely.
  2. Place eggs and sugar in a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment and whisk until pale and fluffy. Combine the flour and almond meal in a bowl and fold into a th egg mixture.
  3. Pour batter into prepared dish and tap gently to level out. Place baking dish on a baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes or until pudding is just cooked but still soft and slightly wobbly in the middle. Remove from oven and stand on a wire rack for 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer baking dish to a heatproof serving platter. Dust with cocoa and spoon over thickened cream to serve.

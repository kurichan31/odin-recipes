Lasagna

Ingredients for Sauce

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (red, yellow, or green), diced (about 3/4/ cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of good quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano or dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garic powder and/or garlic salt
  • 1 tablesppon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablesppon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste) [OPTIONAL]
  • Salt

    • Ingredients for Noodles

  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles (9 unbroken lasagna noodles)
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1/4 pound freshly grated Parmaesan cheese

How to Make Your Stomach Full

  1. Combine all ingredients from the Sauce Ingredients List together. Let this big pot of sauce simmer while you prepare for the noodles.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles. Then mix all the cheeses together (From the noodle ingredient list)
  3. Assemble! First, one layer of noodle on the bottom of the pan. Then one cup of the sauce. Repeat to your heart's desire.
  4. Bake until bubbly in a 350 F degree oven!
